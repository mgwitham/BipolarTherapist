Bipolar approach

How Christopher thinks about bipolar care

Stability isn't the absence of episodes — it's building the awareness and skills to catch them earlier and recover faster. I work with clients on identifying their personal early warning signs, building routines that support mood regulation, and coordinating with prescribers so therapy and medication are working in the same direction.

I've found that people with bipolar often arrive having been treated for depression alone. My work starts by understanding the full picture — including hypomanic periods that might not have seemed like a problem at the time, and the ways those experiences have shaped how you see yourself.

About Christopher

In their own words

You deserve a safe environment where you feel free to express yourself and seek to understand what makes you, you. I am excited about the power of the therapeutic process, and believe that working together we may achieve the change you seek.

My approach centers on building genuine understanding before solutions — the insight usually follows. I work with adolescents and adults, in both English, and bring particular experience with clients who have been misdiagnosed or underserved by previous treatment.
