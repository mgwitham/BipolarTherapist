## Scoring Grid

Use this simple rubric for each reviewed candidate:

- Bipolar relevance: `0-2`
- Trust detail quality: `0-2`
- Practical access clarity: `0-2`
- Outreach clarity: `0-2`
- Launch fit for LA + California telehealth: `0-2`

Interpretation:

- `8-10`: strong launch candidate
- `6-7`: keep in active consideration
- `4-5`: useful backup only
- `0-3`: not a strong first-launch listing
