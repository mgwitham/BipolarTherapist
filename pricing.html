Billing questions

What happens on day 15 if I forget to cancel?

Your card is charged $19 for the first billing month. You can cancel anytime from your dashboard — paid features remain active through the end of the paid period, then your listing returns to free automatically.

Can I cancel mid-month?

Yes. Cancel anytime from your dashboard. You keep paid access through the current billing period and there are no cancellation fees.

Is there a refund policy?

Because the trial is free for 14 days, we don't offer refunds on paid months. If something went wrong, email support@bipolartherapyhub.com and we'll sort it out.

What payment methods are accepted?

All major credit and debit cards via Stripe. Apple Pay and Google Pay are also supported where your browser allows.

What happens if my card declines?

Stripe retries declined charges automatically. If the retry fails, your plan downgrades to Free and your listing stays live. You'll receive an email from Stripe with instructions to update your payment method.
