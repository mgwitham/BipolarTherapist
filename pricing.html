Common questions

FAQ

Does paying improve my ranking?

No. Match results are ranked by fit — bipolar experience, insurance, availability, and location. Free and paid therapists are evaluated by the same rules. Paid adds insight, not placement advantage.

How does the 14-day free trial work?

A card is required to start, but nothing is charged until day 15. Cancel any time before from your dashboard — no email needed — and you'll never be billed.

What happens when I cancel?

Your free listing stays live immediately. You keep access to your analytics history for 30 days after downgrading. Nothing about your public profile changes.

Is patient data included in the analytics?

No. All data is aggregated — only activity counts and source breakdowns. No patient names, contact details, or identities are ever included.

What do I need to get listed?

A valid California therapy license. We verify it instantly against the CA Board of Behavioral Sciences, Board of Psychology, and Medical Board of California. Most clinicians finish in under two minutes. No card needed for the free tier.

Can I switch between monthly and annual?

Yes — any time from your dashboard. If you switch to annual mid-cycle we'll prorate the difference. Annual saves you the equivalent of two months per year.
