Common questions

FAQ

Can I upgrade or downgrade later?

Yes. Upgrade in one click from your dashboard whenever you want. Your free listing stays exactly as it is. Downgrade anytime and your listing keeps running, no interruption.

What if I'm just starting out and don't have many inquiries yet?

Start free. Once patients begin finding you, the dashboard tells you which channels are working so you can lean into them. Most clinicians upgrade after their first few inquiries, when there's something real to learn from.

Does paying improve my ranking?

No. Match results are ranked by fit: bipolar experience, insurance, availability, and location. Free and Insights subscribers are evaluated by the same rules. Insights adds visibility, not placement advantage.

How does the 14-day free trial work?

A card is required to start, but nothing is charged until day 15. We'll email you 3 days before your first charge so it never sneaks up on you. Cancel any time before from your dashboard, no email needed, and you'll never be billed.

What happens when I cancel?

Your free listing stays live immediately. You keep access to your analytics history for 30 days after downgrading. Nothing about your public profile changes.

Is patient data included in the analytics?

No. All data is aggregated. Just activity counts and source breakdowns. No patient names, contact details, or identities are ever included.

What do I need to get listed?

A valid California therapy license. We verify it instantly against the CA Board of Behavioral Sciences, Board of Psychology, and Medical Board of California. Most clinicians finish in under two minutes. No card needed for the free tier.
