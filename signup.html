Common questions

Does it cost anything to be listed?

No. The basic listing is free forever. You can optionally start a 14-day free trial for analytics, a longer bio, and profile-edit tools — $19 per month after trial, cancel anytime. Paid tier has no effect on how your listing ranks.

Do I need to upload a photo or bio right now?

No. The short form is intentionally five fields. After we approve your application, you get a portal link where you can add a headshot, bio, specialties, insurance details, and accepting-patients status at your own pace.

Why California only?

We're intentionally narrow right now: verified bipolar-specialist therapists licensed in California. That focus lets us match patients better and keeps trust high. Other states are on the roadmap.

How do I remove my listing if I change my mind?

Any time. Use the portal to pause or request removal. We email a confirmation link and your listing goes dark the moment you click.
