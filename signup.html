Common questions

How does matching work?

We use your profile information to surface potential fit across factors like specialties, treatment approach, availability, insurance, location, and licensure. We look at overall fit, not any single field alone.

Does completing my profile make a difference?

Yes. More complete profiles give us more context to represent your practice accurately and improve your visibility in relevant matches. Specialties, treatment approach, insurance, and availability are especially useful.

Does it cost anything to be listed?

No. The basic listing is free forever. You can optionally start a 14-day free trial for analytics, a longer bio, and profile-edit tools, $19 per month after trial, cancel anytime. Paid tier has no effect on how your listing ranks. See full pricing →

Why California only?

We're intentionally narrow right now: verified bipolar-specialist therapists licensed in California. That focus lets us match patients better and keeps trust high. Other states are on the roadmap.

How do I remove my listing if I change my mind?

Any time. Use the portal to pause or request removal. We email a confirmation link and your listing goes dark the moment you click.
