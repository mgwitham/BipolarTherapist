    Common questions

    Why am I already listed?

    Your listing was built from your CA DCA license record and publicly visible bipolar-specialty information from your practice site or public directories. Claiming gives you control over it. Removal is one click and instant.

    Does claiming cost anything?

    No. Basic claim is free. If paid features are available for your profile, they appear only after activation so claiming never feels like a billing step.

    What if I can’t access the email on file?

    Use account recovery after selecting your listing. We review that request manually, confirm identity against your California license, and email next steps within one business day.

    What if I’m not yet listed?

    Use our short signup form instead. It is the right path when we do not already have your public profile. Sign up for a new listing →
