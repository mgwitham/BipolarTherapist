    Common questions

    Why am I already listed?

    We auto-added California bipolar-specialist therapists from public records (Board of Behavioral Sciences, Board of Psychology, licensed practice directories). Your public profile exists whether you claim or not — claiming just gives you control over it.

    Does claiming cost anything?

    No. Basic claim is free forever. You can optionally start a 14-day trial for analytics, longer bio, and other paid features — $19/mo after trial, one-click cancel anytime, no charge until day 15.

    What if my on-file email is wrong?

    Pick your listing, then click "Use a different email →" in the confirm panel. Enter the email you actually have access to. We'll send the activation link there and update your on-file email after you click it.

    What if I'm not yet listed?

    Use our short signup form instead — takes sixty seconds, no credit card, approval in 2-3 business days. Sign up for a new listing →
