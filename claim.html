    Common questions

    Does claiming cost anything?

    No. Basic claim is free. If paid features are available for your profile, they appear only after activation so claiming never feels like a billing step.

    What if I can't access the email on file?

    Use account recovery after selecting your listing. We review that request manually, confirm identity against your California license, and email next steps within one business day. The recovery link appears in step 2, below the email destination.

    How long does the activation link last?

    30 minutes. If it expires, just come back to this page and send a fresh one. There's no limit on how many times you can request a new link.
