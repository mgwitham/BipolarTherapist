About BipolarTherapyHub

Who built this. And why.

I've lived with bipolar disorder for over 20 years.

I know what it's like to sit across from a therapist who means well but doesn't quite get it. Who sees the depression but misses the hypomania building underneath. Who treats you like a textbook case when your experience has never felt textbook at all.

I also know what it's like when you finally find the right person. The difference isn't subtle. It changes everything: your stability, your relationships, your sense of what's possible.

It took me years to find that person. Too many wrong fits. Too many restarts. Too much lost time. I built BipolarTherapyHub because no one should have to search that long.

For people living with bipolar disorder

You deserve a therapist who actually gets it.

Finding the right therapist isn't just a preference, it's clinical. Bipolar disorder requires specific expertise: recognizing mixed episodes, supporting medication management, building the kind of stability that holds over years, not just weeks.

Every therapist listed here has been vetted for that specific knowledge. No generalists. No paid placements. Just specialists who have dedicated their practice to bipolar informed care.

You've been managing this long enough. This is where the search gets easier.

For families and loved ones

Bipolar disorder doesn't only affect one person.

If someone you love is struggling, you already know this. The worry, the helplessness, the moments where you're not sure what to do. That's real, and it matters.

This directory is for you too. We can help you find specialists who work with families, who understand caregiver dynamics, and who can help your whole system find more stability. You don't have to figure this out alone either.

For therapists

This is a community built around your work, not around who pays the most.

If you've dedicated your practice to bipolar informed care, you know how rare and demanding that work is. This directory was built to make it easier for the right clients to find you.

We take our vetting seriously because our users are making one of the most consequential decisions of their mental health journey. A listing here signals something most directories can't offer: that you've been recognized for the specific expertise this population needs.

We understand the clinical complexity of this work. Misdiagnosis, medication sensitivity, the long arc of treatment. We're not a lead-generation platform. We're a mission-driven resource, and your reputation is safe here.

BipolarTherapyHub is an independent, mission-driven platform. We don't accept paid rankings. We don't sell your data. We don't take referral fees.

Stability is possible. The right help makes it more likely. That's what we're here for.
