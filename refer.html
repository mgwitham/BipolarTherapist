Why this exists

The referral list in your drawer is probably stale.

If you prescribe for bipolar disorder or see it in your intake screens, you know the problem. The patient needs a therapist who understands mood episodes, and the big directories bury the specialists under thousands of generalists. Half the numbers you hand out reach full practices or dead voicemail.

BipolarTherapyHub is a single-condition directory. Only therapists with documented bipolar experience are listed. Nothing is sold, ranked for pay, or padded with group practice marketing pages.

How therapists are vetted

Three checks before anyone is listed.

  • ✓
    Active California license Every listing is verified against California state licensing records. License numbers are shown on each profile.
  • ✓
    Documented bipolar experience Listings require evidence of bipolar-specific practice from the clinician's own materials. Generalists are not included.
  • ✓
    Individual clinicians only No group practice marketing pages, no treatment center funnels. Every profile is one licensed person your patient can actually reach.
How to refer

Four ways to use it, all under a minute.

  1. Send your patient the city link. Each city page lists the verified specialists nearby with insurance, fees, and telehealth details.
  2. Print the list and hand it over. Every city page has a Print this list button. It prints as a clean one page handout with names, credentials, and the web address for each clinician.
  3. Point them to the match tool. Patients answer a few questions at bipolartherapyhub.com and get a ranked shortlist built around their needs.
  4. Bookmark this page. The directory is maintained continuously, so the list stays current between your appointments.
