Common questions

Why am I listed if I didn't sign up?

Listings are built from public California DCA license records and publicly visible bipolar-specialty information from practice sites or public directories. Removal is instant. Claiming gives you editorial control instead.

What happens after I click the confirmation link?

Your listing is removed immediately. You'll no longer appear in the directory or in match results. You can create a new listing any time via the signup page if you change your mind.

What if I don't have access to the email on file?

Email us directly at support@bipolartherapyhub.com with your full name and CA license number. We'll verify your identity and process the removal manually.
